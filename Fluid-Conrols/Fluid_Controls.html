About Fluid Controls

The Fluid Controls Group has over 40 years of experience in the design and manufacture of instrumentation fittings, valves, manifolds, DIN pipe clamps and SAE flanges. Fluid Controls also manufactures close coupled instrument hook-ups, ball valves under API 607, high pressure needle valves, air manifolds and condensate pots. We are suppliers to onshore and off-shore oil and gas installations, process and power plants and railways in India, USA, Europe and the Middle/Far East. Fluid Controls is system certified with ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001, ISO 18001 and PED, and also has product performance certifications for each product.
